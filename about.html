Kingdom Sound Worship Night

We, as Ambassadors of Christ, have a mandate to CARE for the orphan and to show them God’s LOVE. When God said that He is the Father to the Fatherless, He was calling YOU to be that for Him! God uses His people to fulfil His purposes and to bring His Kingdom to this Earth. Won’t you let Him use YOU so you can discover the blessing that comes from allowing His infilling presence to be poured out through you into the lives of these precious little ones He loves? God Needs YOU to carry His LOVE into the heart of a loved-less child today! So come and serve with LOVE and CARE for His Little Ones and join a movement of LOVE ambassadors! We all need YOU!

Please contact us per email for any further questions.

kingdomsound@gmail.com

Kingdom Sound Worship Night

There will be different activities for kids such as games, face painting, bouncing castle, crazy picture, crafts making along with so many delicious foods, sales of different materials to support the event and also to help some needy orphanages.
